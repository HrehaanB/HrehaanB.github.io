Music × Robotics × Service

Learning that kids can hear, see, and feel.

BoomBot is an educational, percussive robot that plays Boomwhackers to introduce music and robotics—together. Designed to be welcoming, durable, and kid-friendly, BoomBot invites students to pick up an instrument, play along, and discover STEAM through rhythm.

Title I or STEM magnet, small groups or assemblies—BoomBot adapts to meet students where they are and turns curiosity into confidence.

What is BoomBot?

BoomBot is a hands-on learning experience built around a percussive robot and entry-level instruments called Boomwhackers. Students meet a friendly, expressive robot, learn rhythms and melodies, and peek under the hood at circuits, microcontrollers, and code.

Watch a quick demo of BoomBot in action.

Three pillars. One mission.

Every session blends Music (expression), Robotics (curiosity), and Service (inclusion).

Music

Students learn rhythm and melody by performing with the robot. Music becomes a doorway to pattern recognition, timing, and teamwork.

Robotics

Students see how inputs become motion and sound—then try it themselves safely.

Service

Partnering with SEWA, BoomBot brings STEAM to Title I schools and diverse communities.
